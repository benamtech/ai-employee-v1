## Principle

Agents may use the representation that best preserves the software relationships involved. Natural language is a generated interoperability, review, and audit view. It is not the mandatory internal reasoning substrate.

First-class representations include:

- AST/CST, control-flow, data-flow, call, import, dependency, provenance, and knowledge graphs;
- genuine hypergraphs, incidence matrices, duals, tensors, and multilinear operators;
- vectors, embeddings, kernels, eigenspaces, singular vectors, diffusion operators, and geometric/topological structures;
- transition systems, state machines, Petri nets, SAT/SMT models, proof terms, and model-checker traces;
- state-space, queueing, reliability, control, Koopman, DMD/DMDc/EDMDc, and trajectory models when their data prerequisites hold;
- executable schemas, relations, constraints, certificates, and evidence ledgers.

No agent must translate every intermediate shape, vector, matrix, graph, or proof object into prose.

## Proof classes

### P0 — representation calculation

A score, ranking, embedding, diffusion, simulation, spectral mode, or hypothesis without a verified property certificate.

### P1 — formal model-property proof

A machine-checkable certificate of an exact property of an explicit formal model. Examples include:

- a checked eigenpair with an independently recomputed residual;
- an exact connected-component certificate of an incidence structure;
- a solver-certified unsatisfiable constraint set;
- a model-checker proof of unreachability;
- a valid proof term under declared semantics.

P1 requires a claim, model semantics, assumptions, certificate, verifier, tolerances or exact arithmetic boundary, and excluded claims.

### P2 — representation fidelity

Evidence that the formal model corresponds to the exact repository or runtime boundary being discussed. Trace013 implements source-fact and hyperedge membership parity with provenance.

### P3 — executable software evidence

Tests, typecheck, integration, build, database, browser, image, or other execution on an exact candidate.

### P4 — external or production acceptance

Managed database, live provider, target host, accessibility, commercial lifecycle, recovery, trusted signing, pilot, deployment, and production evidence.

No class silently promotes itself. A P1 certificate can satisfy a software gate only when P2 correspondence makes the implication sound.

## Artifact envelope

Every retained representation declares or links to:

```text

parameters, seeds, and tolerances

```

Opaque vectors or impressive matrices without provenance and verification are not authority.

## Hypergraph and eigenvector contract

A hyperedge encodes a genuine joint obligation that pairwise projection would distort. Trace013’s normalized operator is:

```text
Theta = Dv^(-1/2) H W De^(-1) H^T Dv^(-1/2)
L = I - Theta
```

A retained spectral certificate declares positive weights, nonzero represented vertex degrees, finite operator entries, vertex order, eigenvalue, eigenvector, convergence parameters, residual, model digest, correspondence digest, and excluded claims.

A valid eigenvector may prove its exact operator property. It does not automatically prove that its sign partition is the best architecture, that a feature is optimal, that engineering outcomes improve, or that production is ready.
