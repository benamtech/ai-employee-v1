## Current product orientation

AMTECH builds governed persistent AI Employees. The reusable protocol joins identity and assignments, authority and role-safe projection, transport-neutral capabilities/connectors, durable work revisions, approval snapshots, one idempotent effect, terminal receipts, recovery, proof, and bounded Web/SMS/signed Review/MCP Apps/AG-UI projections.

Gmail, QuickBooks, and Stripe are adapters, not the connector ontology.
