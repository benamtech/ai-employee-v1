# Backlog And Non-Goals

Status: complete

## Not MVP

Do not build before the real whole-product loop works:

- Live-money Stripe mode.
- Full billing/paywall enforcement for AMTECH subscriptions.
- Google Calendar write integration.
- Voice calls.
- Multi-human org management.
- Multi-employee coordination.
- Public skill marketplace.
- Broad connector marketplace.
- Customer-facing intake widget.
- Browser-use workflows.
- Autonomous third-party sends without approval.
- Autonomous spending/refunds.
- Raw Hermes dashboard as owner UI.
- Native mobile apps.
- Complex notification batching.

## Required For MVP

These are not backlog:

- real Gmail send;
- real Gmail reply listener;
- real PDF estimate;
- real Stripe Connect test-mode setup;
- real Stripe deposit invoice/payment URL;
- real Stripe webhook processing;
- internal job reminder.

## Fast Follow

After MVP:

- Google Calendar connection.
- Drive connector for past estimates/photos.
- Customer-facing intake widget.
- Stripe live-mode pilot checklist.
- Contractor-specific estimate template library.
- Hosted self-bootstrapping Estimate skill.
- Invoicing beyond deposits.
- Multi-user office/admin roles.
- Multi-employee org model.
- Voice-walk estimate flow.
- Capability/paywall enforcement.

## Kill Criteria

Cut or defer anything that:

- delays the real estimate -> email -> reply -> deposit loop;
- requires the owner to understand infrastructure;
- creates a second owner-facing assistant besides the employee;
- duplicates Hermes primitives;
- weakens approval gates;
- replaces provider-backed proof with theater.
