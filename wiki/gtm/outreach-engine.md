# GTM - Outreach Engine (Free Estimator First, Contractor Beachhead)

**Status: complete** · _Revised 2026-06-29 for the current motion: outreach drives into the Employee-like onboarding/consulting-sales surface and a real work object, then the tuned package + live Employee. See [`consulting-sales-surface.md`](consulting-sales-surface.md) and [`../offers/wedge-offers.md`](../offers/wedge-offers.md)._

## 30-day target

Get **30–40 contractors through a completed free-estimator attempt** (an estimate produced from their own job), convert **8–15 tuned Estimate packages ($300)**, and **provision 1–3 paid live Employee setups** from paint/landscape contractors, working the ~350 names already in hand. The first cash path is not a big new list — it is getting real jobs into the public estimator, then following up where the output creates trust. **You are selling the Employee relationship; the tuned package is paid discovery that installs the pricing brain.**

## Daily activity target (after Week 1)

- 60 targeted emails / follow-ups to with-website rows using "We give contractors free estimates."
- 30 call attempts to no-website or high-intent rows (early AM / evening for owners).
- 8 completed estimator attempts reviewed and triaged.
- 5 referral asks from prior demos/customers.
- 1 reusable asset improved (better `contractor_estimator` package, tighter interview script, a new Work Surface deliverable touch).

## Funnel math (conservative 30-day model)

- ~350 names enriched + topped up by trade.
- ~1,000 outbound touches over the month.
- ~60–100 estimator starts.
- ~30–40 completed first-work loops on their own jobs.
- ~10–15 tuned packages at $300 = **$3,000–$4,500**.
- ~2–3 Employee setups at $750 = **$1,500–$2,250 + the first monthly**.

**Cash target, first 30 days: ~$4,500–$7,000 collected**, plus 1–3 recurring Employees started. If no tuned package or Employee closes after 40 real first-work loops, fix the demo (is it on *their* job, in the real surface?) or narrow to one sub-trade — don't switch segments.

## List method — enrich, don't rebuild

You already have the names. This week:

1. Pull every painter / landscaper / hardscaper out of the ~350 into a working sheet. Columns: owner name, phone, website/email, trade, city, source, first-work status, tuned-package status, Employee status, objection.
2. Tag trade from the business name + a 10-second search. Flag **with-website rows as estimator-email-first** and **no-website phone rows as guided-estimator-call-first**.
3. Top up only if a town's paint/landscape count is thin: Google Maps ("painters near [town]", "landscaping [town]"), local trade groups, supply-house boards.
4. Prioritize: visible job photos, owner-operated, 1–10 person, doing frequent estimates.

## Call/message variants

**Cold call (phone-only list):** "I work with painters and landscapers — we give contractors one free estimate using an AI employee built for job notes and photos. You pick one real job, we draft it with assumptions, and you tell us what is right or wrong. No signup. Worth trying on the next one?"

**Email (with-website list):** use the canonical copy in [`free-estimator-funnel.md`](free-estimator-funnel.md): subject **"We give contractors free estimates."**

**Hiring trigger:** "Saw you're hiring an estimator/office person. Before you do — I can stand up an AI employee that writes your estimates within ~5% and handles the send/follow-up behind your approval, so you know what to automate vs. hire."

## Follow-up schedule

- Day 0: email estimator CTA for web rows; call no-website rows.
- Day 1: callback at their estimate time (early AM / evening), or phone follow-up to the email.
- Day 3: send a reminder with the concrete ask: "pick one job you already need to estimate."
- Day 7: one more call/email: "Want me to walk you through one estimate free so you can see the whole loop?"
- Day 14: break-up: "Should I close this out, or is there a job you want your employee to try?"

## Compliance guardrails

- **No cold texting.** Phone + email outbound only.
- B2B cold email includes accurate identity, business purpose, and opt-out.
- Phone calls use a business number and respect DNC/opt-out.
- Trade-group posts are value-first and follow group rules.
- **Anything leaving the customer's business** (estimates, invoices, follow-up) stays behind one-tap owner approval — the trust signature of the product, enforced structurally by deliverable type ([`../principle-deliverable-driven-surfaces.md`](../principle-deliverable-driven-surfaces.md)). Customer follow-up only to existing/inbound leads — never cold outbound on the customer's behalf (TCPA).

## Weekly experiment cadence

- **Week 1:** paint vs. landscape — which sub-trade books demos faster? Test cold-call opener wording.
- **Week 2:** keep the winning sub-trade/opener; add the with-website email + real-artifact motion.
- **Week 3:** add local in-person demos; provision the first paid Employee for a happy first-work buyer.
- **Week 4:** push paid conversions (tuned package + Employee), take one pilot through the full provider-accepted loop, collect before/after proof, and decide whether to deepen paint/landscape or add the next trade. Do **not** open bookkeepers until the doc-sorter skill exists (~week 8–12).
