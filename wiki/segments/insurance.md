# Segment — Independent Insurance Agencies (Parked)

**Status: complete** · _Evaluated, parked. Our beachhead is paint/landscape contractors; see [`../00-decision.md`](../00-decision.md)._

A credible later vertical, parked for now because regulation slows the first sale.

## What's there

Independent agencies have a real admin trap: high email volume, renewals, endorsements, certificates, policy changes, file maintenance. An insurance professional reports ~80 emails/day plus calls, renewals, and new business, "not enough time in the day" [S011]; CSR work covers collecting documents, renewal/endorsement review, certs, and file upkeep [S012].

## Skill-shaped tasks (if revisited)

Certificate/renewal request prep for licensed-staff approval; renewal packet assembly; inbox triage by urgency. All **prep-only** — never "AI issues certificates."

## Substitute cost

CSR / account manager: ~$25/hr or roughly $40–75k/yr depending on role and market [S013, S014]. Budget exists.

## Why parked

Whole-product risk is high: PII, E&O exposure, licensing, carrier rules, and state regulation make a fast first sale hard, and licensed staff (not just the principal) control workflow adoption. Phone-reachable and fundable, but the trust path is slower than contractors. Revisit once AMTECH has contractor proof and a tighter prep-only, compliance-safe package.
