# Segment — Property Management, Small (Parked)

**Status: complete** · _Evaluated, parked. Our beachhead is paint/landscape contractors; see [`../00-decision.md`](../00-decision.md)._

Strong recurring workflows, parked behind contractors mainly on support burden and the lack of an owned wedge skill.

## What's there

Small PM has relentless coordination pain: tenant requests, owner updates, vendor assignment, photos, status, documentation. Operators ask how overwhelming many properties are [S015], flag overloaded assistant workloads [S016], and describe solo-managing 47 units with tenant requests, contractor assignment, and documented communication [S017]; BiggerPockets has "Overwhelmed by frequent tenant repair requests" [S018].

## Skill-shaped tasks (if revisited)

Maintenance-request triage pack (summarize, classify urgency, vendor category, draft owner/tenant update); weekly owner digest; vendor-quote comparison. Internal triage first — no autonomous tenant notices (fair-housing/access rules).

## Substitute cost

Assistant PM / coordinator: roughly high-$30k–$55k/yr [S019, S020]. Recurring rent revenue = steady budget.

## Why parked

The workflow fits an always-on Employee well, but it leans on live, 24/7 tenant/vendor urgency that can create heavy support load for a solo founder, and AMTECH owns no PM wedge skill (the Estimate asset is contractor-fit). A genuinely strong later pin — revisit after contractors, with a triage-prep package and clear non-emergency boundaries.
