Real admin pain, parked because the market is saturated with AI/lead-gen pitches and carries heavy TCPA sensitivity.

## What's there

Recurring but noisy: lead follow-up, CRM updates, transaction coordination, social, admin. Agents describe forgetting/manual follow-up [S025], ask how others handle day-to-day admin (transaction coordination, CRM, social, lead gen) [S026], and small agencies get overwhelmed enough to consider a VA [S027].

## Skill-shaped tasks (if revisited)

Listing/research prep packs, transaction checklists, CRM cleanup/follow-up *planners* (draft, don't send) — for licensed review, no autonomous messaging.

## Substitute cost

Transaction coordinator / VA / ISA: TC economics around $300–500/file or ~$51k/yr in some markets [S028].

## Why parked

The pain is real, but buyers are trained to expect lead/revenue claims, the market is extremely crowded with CRMs, lead-gen, and AI pitches [S025–S028], and TCPA/fair-housing/licensing add friction. It clashes with AMTECH's internal-back-office, no-cold-outbound wedge. Parked; only warm-intro experiments if ever revisited.
